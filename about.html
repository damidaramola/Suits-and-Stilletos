Who are we?

Suits and Stilletos is an educational resources platform that aims to help young undergraduates navigate their career path. We are passionate about reducing the pressure of post-graduate days by providing support to our users. We provide information on the latest networking event, mentoring sessions from industry proffesionals, and links to courses that will get you ready for the workplace!
